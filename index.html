Research Interests

Runqi Lin is a currently PhD student supervised by Prof. Tongliang Liu at The University of Sydney. His research interests lie in adversarial training, and working on addressing catastrophic overfitting in single-step adversarial training. If you have any ideas or meet any problems, you're more than welcome to discuss them.
