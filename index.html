About Me

I am currently a third-year PhD student at the University of Sydney, supervised by Prof. Tongliang Liu. Before this, I received my Bachelor's degree in Data Science & Big Data Technology from Beijing Information Science and Technology University (BISTU) in 2021.
My research interests lie in trustworthy machine learning, with a particular emphasis on adversarial robustness and generalization ability.
